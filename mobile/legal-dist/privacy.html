Surround Speaker

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 June 2026
PrivacyTerms

Surround Speaker ("the app") turns your phone into a synchronized satellite speaker for audio playing on a host computer on your local Wi-Fi network. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Summary

The app collects no personal data, has no accounts, contains no ads, and uses no analytics or third-party tracking. Everything happens on your own devices and your local network.

What the app accesses

  • Camera — used only to scan a host's QR code so the app knows which computer to connect to. The image is processed on your device in the moment and is not stored, recorded, or transmitted.
  • Local network — the app discovers and connects to a host computer on the same Wi-Fi to receive synchronized audio. This communication stays on your local network; it is not sent to us or any third party.
  • Audio playback — the app plays audio streamed from the host. It does not record, store, or upload audio.
  • Local settings — the last host address and a display name you choose are stored only on your device so you don't retype them. Uninstalling the app clears them.

What we do not collect

We do not collect names, emails, contacts, location, advertising identifiers, usage analytics, or any audio. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
