## Full description

Surround Speaker turns the phones around you into one synchronized sound
system. Play music on your computer, and every phone that joins becomes a
satellite speaker — left, right, mono, or full stereo — all playing the same
moment in time for a real surround effect across the room.

No accounts. No sign-up. Just open the app on the same Wi-Fi as the host
computer, scan its QR code (or let the app find it automatically), and tap to
join.

WHY IT STAYS IN SYNC
Independent phones have independent clocks and variable Wi-Fi latency, so naive
streaming drifts. Surround locks every device to a shared clock and a single
start instant, then gently corrects drift — so the room plays as one.

FEATURES
• Join in seconds — scan a QR code or auto-discover the host on your Wi-Fi
• Synchronized playback across many phones at once
• Per-phone role: stereo, left, right, or mono for a surround field
• Per-phone volume and fine delay trim to line everything up
• Keeps the screen awake while playing
• No account, no ads, no tracking — everything stays on your local network

Surround Speaker is the companion app for the Surround host (macOS / Windows).
Start the host on your computer, and bring the room to life.
