# Privacy Policy — Surround Speaker

**Effective date:** 14 June 2026

Surround Speaker ("the app") turns your phone into a synchronized satellite
speaker for audio playing on a host computer on your local Wi-Fi network. This
policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

## Summary

The app collects **no personal data**, has **no accounts**, contains **no ads**,
and uses **no analytics or third-party tracking**. Everything happens on your
own devices and your local network.

## What the app accesses

- **Camera** — used only to scan a host's QR code so the app knows which
  computer to connect to. The camera image is processed on your device in the
  moment and is **not stored, recorded, or transmitted**.
- **Local network** — the app discovers and connects to a host computer on the
  same Wi-Fi to receive synchronized audio. This communication stays on your
  local network; it is not sent to us or any third party.
- **Audio playback** — the app **plays** audio streamed from the host. It does
  **not** record, store, or upload audio.
- **Local settings** — the last host address and a display name you choose are
  stored **only on your device** (local storage) so you don't retype them. You
  can clear them by uninstalling the app.

## What we do not collect

We do not collect names, emails, contacts, location, advertising identifiers,
usage analytics, or any audio. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from
anyone.
